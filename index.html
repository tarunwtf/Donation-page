Our Mission

We see prevention, treatment, rehabilitation, and promotion as the four pillars that our foundation stands upon while embarking on a journey to take mental health to every household.

Our Vision

We believe in building a holistic model of mental health care that is inclusive of social, economic, political, and cultural factors to make mental health accessible for all communities.

About Us

Mental health is a non-profit organization working to bridge the intervention gap, advance in education and training in mental health, bust stigma, and provide access to quality mental health.
